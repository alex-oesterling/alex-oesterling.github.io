About Me üëÄ

I am a PhD student at Harvard under the mentorship of Dr. Flavio du Pin Calmon and Dr. Hima Lakkaraju and am supported by the NSF Graduate Student Research Fellowship.

I am broadly interested in fair, interpretable, and trustworthy machine learning, and my current projects apply signal processing and information theoretic tools to problems in interpretability, fairness, and regulation. Although I love digging into theory, I am an engineer by training and hope to use my research to design actionable solutions for society.

Previously, I worked as an intern at Apple Research with the AI/ML Visualization Team under the amazing mentorship of Fred Hohman.
